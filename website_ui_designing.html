Website UI Designing

Designing websites that offer a great user experience is my goal. I create clean, responsive, and user-friendly web interfaces that ensure smooth navigation and accessibility. As a beginner in the industry, I am constantly learning and improving my design approach to create websites that are both attractive and easy to use.

  • I create wireframes and convert them into UI designs for a structured and efficient design flow.
  • I use Balsamiq and Figma to design wireframes, ensuring a strong foundation for the final UI.
  • Sometimes, I focus on specific sections like banner sections, navigation, and hero sections for quick prototypes.
  • I work on responsive designs that look great on both desktop and mobile devices.
  • I continuously explore new design trends to enhance my skills and creativity.
